What Is a Prediction Market?

A prediction market is a financial exchange where participants buy and sell contracts tied to the outcome of future events. Unlike traditional markets that trade existing assets, prediction markets enable you to trade on future outcomes — from product launches to election results.

The Core Principle: Price = Probability

Each contract trades between $0.00 and $1.00, with the price directly reflecting the market's collective belief in the event occurring:

  • • $0.75 share price = 75% probability the event happens
  • • $0.25 share price = 25% probability the event happens
  • • If the event occurs, YES shares pay $1.00 each
  • • If the event doesn't occur, NO shares pay $1.00 each
