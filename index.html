What Are Prediction Markets?

A prediction market is a trading platform where participants buy and sell shares representing the outcome of future events. Unlike traditional stock markets that reflect past performance, prediction markets aggregate collective wisdom to forecast what will happen.

Core Concept: Price = Probability

Each share trades between $0.00 and $1.00, and the current market price directly represents the crowd's estimated probability of the event occurring.

$0.75 = 75% probability
$0.30 = 30% probability
$0.05 = 5% probability

If the event occurs: Each "Yes" share pays out $1.00
If the event fails: "Yes" shares are worth $0.00

This mechanism incentivizes accurate forecasting: traders profit by moving prices toward true probabilities. The result is a dynamic, real-time consensus that often outperforms traditional polls and expert predictions.
