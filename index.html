What is a Prediction Market?

A prediction market is a crowdsourced intelligence platform where participants buy and sell contracts tied to the outcome of future events. The market price of each contract reflects the collective wisdom of all participants‚Äîessentially a real-time probability estimate.

Whether you're forecasting tech launches, geopolitical outcomes, or financial metrics, prediction markets aggregate dispersed knowledge into a single, transparent price signal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are markets resolved?

Markets are resolved by PredictionMart's independent verification team using authoritative sources (official announcements, verified data providers, consensus media reports). In rare disputes, a decentralized oracle system provides final arbitration.

What happens if I'm wrong?

If the event resolves against your position, your shares become worthless and you lose your investment in that contract. This is why proper research and risk management are essential. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.

Can I sell my shares before the event resolves?

Yes! You can buy and sell shares at any time before market close. If the price moves in your favor, you can lock in profits early. If sentiment shifts against you, you can exit to minimize losses.

Is this gambling or investing?

Prediction markets are information aggregation mechanisms, not pure gambling. Success requires research, analysis, and disciplined risk management‚Äî similar to trading stocks or commodities. However, all trading involves risk, and regulatory treatment varies by jurisdiction.

What fees does PredictionMart charge?

We charge a small transaction fee (typically 2-5%) on winning positions when markets resolve. There are no monthly fees, and deposits/withdrawals incur only standard payment processing costs.
